🟣 ASTY – Arbitrage Strategy Protocol

🚀 The ASTY Effect  
ASTY is not built on hype.

By combining trading activity with a defined fee system, ASTY transforms market activity into a self-reinforcing ecosystem.

Activity → Fees → Burn → Reinvestment → Stronger System

---

⚙️ How It Works  

Every transaction generates value.

A fixed 1% fee applies to:
- Buys
- Sells
- Wallet transfers

This fee is automatically allocated:

- 40% Burned → reduces circulating supply  
- 40% Reinvested → strengthens liquidity pools  
- 20% Treasury → stored in SOL or USDC for reserves

🔄 The ASTY Effect  

The system operates as a continuous cycle:

1. Trading activity generates fees  
2. Fees trigger burn, reinvestment, and treasury growth  
3. Supply decreases over time  
4. Stronger pools improve capital efficiency  
5. Improved efficiency enables more activity  

The cycle repeats and compounds.

📊 What is Arbitrage  

Arbitrage means capturing price differences across markets.

Example:
- Buy an asset cheaper in one market  
- Sell it higher in another  
- Keep the difference  

In crypto, these opportunities exist due to:
- Fast-moving markets  
- Liquidity differences  
- Unsynced pricing between pools  

ASTY is designed to operate within this environment.
